A Growing Urban Health Crisis

Air pollution is a silent threat impacting millions. Our cities are at a critical point, and effective action requires reliable, high-resolution data.

99%

of the world's population breathes air that exceeds WHO guideline limits.

1.6M

premature deaths in India annually are attributed to air pollution.

Rapid urbanization and climate change are exacerbating pollution levels.

Powered by NASA Earth Observation Data

We leverage NASA's free, global, and validated data to get a clear picture of air quality. This powerful resource is the foundation of our solution.

Aura OMI & MODIS

Measures key pollutants like NO₂, Ozone, and Aerosol Optical Depth (a proxy for PM₂.₅).

Sentinel / Landsat

Provides insights into land use, vegetation, and the formation of urban heat islands.

The Hackronix Framework

Our approach transforms raw data into meaningful change through a clear, three-step process designed to create healthier communities.

Data Collection

Aggregating satellite imagery and ground sensor data.

Analysis

Identifying pollution hotspots and trends.

Actionable Insights

Providing clear recommendations for urban planners.

From Data to Real-World Change

Our insights directly inform tangible interventions that make a difference in people's lives.

Traffic Management Zones

Restrict high-emission vehicles in pollution hotspots to immediately improve air quality.

Green Corridors

Guide targeted tree planting and green space development in the most critical areas.

Public Health Alerts

Notify sensitive groups, like the elderly and children, during high-pollution events.

Building a Breathable Future

Our vision extends beyond monitoring. We aim to create a predictive and integrated system for proactive urban management.

Higher Accuracy: Combine satellite data with ground-based IoT sensors for hyper-local insights.

Predictive Forecasting: Use machine learning to forecast pollution events 24-48 hours in advance.

Seamless Integration: Integrate our platform with existing urban planning apps and smart city dashboards.
